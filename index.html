About Us

At Nilgiri's fresh bakes, we're passionate about crafting delicious, freshly baked goods that bring joy to our community. From our signature sourdough bread to our decadent pastries, each item is made with the finest ingredients and a dedication to traditional baking methods. Our story began with Prasanna's love for baking and a dream to share their creations with others. Over the years, we've grown from a small, home-based operation to a beloved local bakery. We are committed to using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible and creating a welcoming space for everyone.
